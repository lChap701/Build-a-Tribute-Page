Timeline

  • 1989 - Tim creates the World Wide Web
  • 1990 - Tim writes the first web client, which was httpd, and server, which was called WorldWideWeb
  • 1991 to 1993 - Tim works on the design of the Web
  • 1994 - Tim creates and becomes the Director of the W3C
  • 1999 - Tim becomes the first holder of the 3Com chair at the laboratory for Computer Science at MIT and his book Weaving the Web is published
  • 2009 - Tim creates and becomes the Director of the World Wide Web Foundation
